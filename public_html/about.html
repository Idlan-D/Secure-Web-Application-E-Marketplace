Why choose us ?

Welcome to Lan Bakery, your go-to spot for delicious homemade cakes and pastries. Since day one, we've been passionate about baking treats that bring people together – whether it's a slice of our rich butter cake, a smooth, creamy cheesecake, or a freshly baked muffin straight from the oven.Every item at Lan Bakery is made with care, using quality, halal-certified ingredients and time-honored recipes that remind you of home. We believe that good food has the power to create sweet memories – and that’s exactly what we aim to do with every bite.Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just treating yourself, thank you for letting us be a part of your day. At Lan Bakery, happiness is always baked in.
